About Us

A Legacy of Learning,
A Future of Possibility

Founded in 1999, Luminary Academy stands at the intersection of tradition and innovation. Our mission is simple: to nurture curious minds, build confident characters, and prepare every student for a life of purpose and achievement.

With state-of-the-art facilities, an internationally certified curriculum, and a dedicated faculty of 320+ educators, we provide an environment where every student is known, supported, and challenged to excel.
